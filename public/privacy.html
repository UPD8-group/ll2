Privacy Policy

Listing Lens · The UPD8 Group Pty Ltd · Last updated February 2026

The short version

No account required. We never ask for your name or email.

Screenshots deleted within 15 minutes — automatically, always, no exceptions.

Your report goes to your browser. We keep no copy.

Payment processed by Stripe. We never see your card details.

No tracking cookies. No data sales. No advertisers. Ever.

01

Who we are

Listing Lens is a product of The UPD8 Group Pty Ltd, an Australian company. We built Listing Lens on a single principle: you should be able to get an intelligent vehicle analysis without creating an account, handing over personal details, or worrying about what happens to your data afterwards.

02

Your screenshots

When you upload listing screenshots, they are held in encrypted temporary storage (Netlify Blobs) for the sole purpose of generating your report.

Screenshots are deleted immediately after your report is generated, and automatically deleted after 15 minutes regardless. This is enforced at the system level — not a manual process.

Your screenshots are never backed up, never used to train AI models, and never shared with any third party except the AI provider that analyses them to generate your report (Anthropic — see section 06).

03

Your report

Your report is generated by our AI and transmitted directly to your browser session. We do not store, log, or retain a copy of your report on our servers. Once it reaches your browser, it exists only there.

If you close the tab or clear your session, the report is gone. This is by design — your data should belong to you, not us.

04

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. — one of the world's most trusted payment platforms. We never see, handle, store, or have access to your card number, CVV, expiry date, or any payment credentials.

Stripe provides us only with a payment confirmation status and a transaction reference number. That is all we receive.

05

Personal information

We collect none. There is no account system. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information at any point in the process.

No account. No profile. No history. The service is designed so that we genuinely cannot identify who used it.

06

AI provider

Your screenshots are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to this processing: anthropic.com/privacy.

Anthropic does not use inputs from paid API customers to train its models by default. Your screenshots are processed to generate your report and are not retained by Anthropic for training purposes.

07

Cookies and tracking

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of behavioural tracking. We do not have advertisers. We do not sell data. We do not share data with data brokers.

Basic session functionality may use browser storage to hold your report temporarily within your tab. This data never leaves your device.

08

Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate Listing Lens:

Stripe Secure payment processing. Stripe never shares card details with us.
Anthropic AI report generation via the Claude API. Screenshots sent for analysis only.
Netlify Hosting, serverless functions, and encrypted temporary file storage.
09

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will not make changes that reduce your privacy protections without clear notice.

10

Contact

Privacy questions or concerns? We aim to respond within 5 business days.

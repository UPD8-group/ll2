The short version

✓ No account required. We never ask for your name or email.

✓ We don't use cookies for tracking. We don't sell data. We don't have advertisers.

Who we are

Listing Lens is a product of The UPD8 Group Pty Ltd, based in Australia.

Screenshots

Your screenshots are held in encrypted temporary storage (Netlify Blobs) for a maximum of 15 minutes. They are deleted immediately after your report is generated, and automatically deleted after 15 minutes regardless. This deletion is enforced by the system.

Your screenshots are never backed up, never shared with third parties (other than the AI provider that analyses them), and never used for any purpose other than generating your report.

Reports

Your report is generated by our AI and transmitted directly to your browser. We do not store, log, or retain a copy of your report.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We never see, handle, or store your card number, CVV, or any payment credentials. Stripe provides us only with a payment confirmation and transaction reference.

Personal information

We collect none. There is no account system. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or tracking pixels.

AI provider

Your screenshots are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy. Anthropic does not use API inputs from paid API customers to train its models by default.

Third-party services

Stripe — payment processing.
Anthropic — AI report generation.
Netlify Blobs — temporary encrypted storage (15-minute maximum).
Netlify — hosting and serverless functions.

Contact

Privacy questions: listinglens@upd8.group. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
